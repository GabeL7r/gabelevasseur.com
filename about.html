Nuclear Shift Test Engineer

I completed a two-year training program to become a certified Nuclear Shift Test Engineer (STE). Software Engineer

I learned. I adapted. I thrived.

Senior DevOps Engineer

A growth start-up with a noble mission: To Improve the Health and Happiness of the World. At the time I applied there was only one Engineering position listed on their website: DevOps Engineer. Something I had limited experience with. I applied anyways.


I got the job.


What is service discovery? What is a service mesh? What is the difference between provisioning and configuration? These terms were foreign to me. After being immersed into DevOps these, and many other, terms are now second nature.

I helped decentralized DevOps to give developers more ownership and access to their infrastructure. I have implemented security tools to deter malicious users.
